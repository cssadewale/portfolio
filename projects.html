CBT System

"Built by a tutor, for tutors. AI-augmented. Zero budget. Zero compromise."

  • Solution Full-stack exam platform: CSV upload, timed sessions, scientific calculator, anti-cheat detection, analytics, certificates, WhatsApp sharing.
