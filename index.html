The Story Behind the Work
Educator · Builder · Data Scientist

I Did Not Start in Tech.
I Started in a Classroom.

For over 15 years, I stood in Nigerian secondary school classrooms teaching Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry — from JSS1 all the way to SSS3. Teaching gave me something no university course can: the ability to read a room, break down complexity, and understand exactly why someone is not understanding something. That instinct lives in everything I build today.

The day I decided to stop complaining about broken tools and start building replacements changed everything. CBT Pro was born not in a co-working space with fast Wi-Fi — but driven by a clear understanding of the problem, leveraged through AI tools, with zero budget and one clear goal: give my students a better exam experience. Today that platform is live and used by real students. That experience proved something I now live by: a clear thinker with the right AI tools is unstoppable in any domain.

My formal tech journey began with the 3MTT programme in 2025 — Data Science track. That spark accelerated everything. I am not just a data scientist who once used HTML and CSS. I am an AI-Augmented Solutions Developer — someone who uses clear thinking and AI assistance to operate confidently across any technical domain. I have deployed 12 projects across ML, EdTech, NLP, HR analytics, and logistics. I am enrolled in WorldQuant University's Applied Data Science Lab, and I am growing deliberately into every domain that serves the people I build for.

"Data science taught me that if you can think through a problem clearly, you can find a path to a solution — even in unfamiliar territory."
