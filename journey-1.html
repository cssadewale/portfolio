2009 — Where Everything Began
2009 — Present (Still Teaching)15+ Years Active
Classroom Teacher — Nursery Through Secondary School
Multiple Nigerian Schools · Lagos & Ogun State
First classroom: Chrisville Schools, 2009. A JSS class. Chalk, a blackboard, and more questions from students than I had been trained to answer. Since then: His Marvellous Grace, Fredaks Group, Anchor Heights, Dave Model School, Lifeline, De Glorious College, Chrisville, Angel High School, God of Seed Academy, and others. I have taught at every level — Nursery, Primary, and Secondary (currently in secondary school, JSS1–SSS3). Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science. Thousands of students.

These classrooms trained me in the two most important skills any data scientist can have: asking the right questions, and genuinely caring whether your audience understands what you are showing them. Fifteen years later, I am still teaching. The classroom and the data science are not two separate lives. They have always been the same work, waiting for the right tools.
